Chart of Accounts is a chronological list of all accounts. It shows any code numbers (if business uses codes to identify a transaction) and Balance amounts of the accounts.

Chart of Accounts is used by businesses with computerized accounting system, as it provides on-time balances of each and every accounts. Business with manual recording system makes Trial Balance at every end of financial accounting period, or when required.
