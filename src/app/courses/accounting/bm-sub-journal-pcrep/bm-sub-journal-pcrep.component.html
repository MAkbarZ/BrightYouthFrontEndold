The custodian of petty cash safe submits all the payment documents to finance department.

Then, the finance department issues a check in the name of petty cash custodian.

The petty cash custodian withdraws the check from bank and puts the amount back into the petty cash safe.

The balance amount is reset again. This process is called “Replenishment” process.
