A business records all the petty transactions (transactions in small amounts) into Petty Cash Book.

Most businesses keep a sum of amount into a separate safe which, then, is used to pay for small transactions, like cash to coffee shop for a guest, urgent cleaning expenses, small stationary purchases, taxi expenses, etc.
