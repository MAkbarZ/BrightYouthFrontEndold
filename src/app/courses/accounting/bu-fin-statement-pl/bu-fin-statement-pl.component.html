"Statement of Profit and Loss" is prepared to show the total revenue, total expenditures and the profit or loss amounts during a period of time.

A Statement of Profit and Loss, which is also called “Income statement”, shows performance of a business for the period this statement has been prepared.

If the profit is higher, then the business is known as high performant business.
