Sales

Total Sales or just Sales is also called Revenue recorded after issuing an Invoice to a Customer for the total sales amount.

Net Sales

Net Sales is the amount calculated after deducting all selling expenses from total sales. Such as, sales return, sales discount, carriage out-wards, etc.

Gross Profit

Gross Profit is the amount calculated after deducting all purchases and production expenses from Net Sales. Such as, Cost of Goods sold, carriage in-wards, overheads, etc.

Net Income

Net Income also called Net Profit, is the amount calculated after deducting all non-selling expenses and taxes from Gross Profit.
