Ledger is a book of account used to systematically transfer transaction impacts from journal.

Note:

  • Balance (C/F) referred to as “Balance Carried Forward” is also called “Closing Balance”. This balance is transferred or carried forward to next period.
  • Balance (B/F) referred to as “Balance Brought Forward” is also called “Opening Balance”. This balance has been transferred from Previous Period.
