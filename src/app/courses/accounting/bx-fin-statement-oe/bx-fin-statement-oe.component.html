Statement Of Owner’s Equity is prepared to show the equity of owner(s) of a business and changes into those equities during a reporting period.

The “Total Net Equity Amount” showed in the Statement Of Owner’s Equity is the amount of “Net Worth” of the business.


Below is a format for Statement Of Owner’s Equity.

XYZ COMPANY
STATEMENT OF OWNER’S EQUITY
